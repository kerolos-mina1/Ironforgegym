 Our Story

Built by Athletes.
For Athletes.

IronForge was founded in 2014 by former competitive powerlifter Marcus Cole with a single conviction: every person deserves access to serious training in a serious environment.

We built this gym on the factory floor aesthetic — raw iron, honest work, zero ego. From first-timers to seasoned competitors, IronForge is where real transformation happens.
